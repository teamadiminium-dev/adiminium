Description

Writing is an art, but sometimes it requires a bit of math. The Word & Character Counter is a comprehensive writing assistant that analyzes your text in real-time. It goes beyond simple counting; it provides insights into the structure and length of your content. Whether you are a student meeting an essay word count, a social media manager crafting the perfect tweet, or a blogger optimizing for reading time, this tool ensures you hit the mark every time. It operates entirely in your browser, guaranteeing your privacy.

What It Does

Counts words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs while estimating reading and speaking times.

How To Use

  • Type or Paste: Enter your text into the main area. Analysis happens instantly as you type.
  • View Stats: Check the cards above for Word, Character, Sentence, and Paragraph counts.
  • Check Time: Look at the toolbar to see estimated Reading and Speaking times.

Who It Is For

Writers, editors, students, SEO specialists, and social media content creators.

Freqently Asked Questions

Does it count spaces?

The "Character" count typically includes spaces. We count every keystroke to give you the precise length of your string.

How is reading time calculated?

We use the industry standard of 200 words per minute (WPM) to estimate how long an average adult takes to read the text silently.

Is my text saved?

No. All calculations are performed locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device.
