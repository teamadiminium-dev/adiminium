Case Converter

Description

Messy text can be a headache, but fixing it doesn't have to be. The Case Converter is your one-click solution for text formatting. Maybe you accidentally left Caps Lock on, or you need to standardize a list of names. Instead of retyping everything, simply paste your text here and transform it. We support standard formats like Uppercase and Lowercase, as well as smarter options like Title Case (for headlines) and Sentence Case (for fixing grammar).

What It Does

Instantly changes the capitalization style of your text block to your chosen format.

How To Use

  • Mismatched Text: Paste any text into the input field.
  • Choose Style: Click buttons like "UPPERCASE" or "Title Case" to apply the change immediately.
  • Copy: Use the "Copy" button to grab the cleaned-up result.

Who It Is For

Data entry clerks, programmers cleaning database inputs, students, and writers.

Freqently Asked Questions

What is Title Case?

Title Case capitalizes the first letter of every major word, making it perfect for headers, book titles, and naming conventions.

Does Sentence Case fix grammar?

It capitalizes the first letter of sentences (defined by periods). It won't full grammar check your work, but it fixes the capitalization structure.

What is Alternating Case?

It switches between lower and uppercase (e.g., "hElLo wOrLd"), often used for stylistic emphasis or internet memes.
