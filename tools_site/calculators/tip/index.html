Description

Dining out with friends should be fun, not a math test. The Tip Calculator eliminates the awkward moments at the end of a meal when the bill arrives. It helps you quickly calculate the appropriate gratuity based on your satisfaction and service level, and then splits the total evenly among everyone at the table. No more scribbling on napkins or guessing percentages. This tool ensures servers are tipped fairly and everyone pays their fair share.

What It Does

Calculates the tip amount based on a percentage of the bill and divides the total (Bill + Tip) by the number of people.

How To Use

  • Bill Amount: Enter the subtotal from your receipt.
  • Tip %: Enter the percentage you wish to tip (standard is often 15-20%).
  • People: Enter how many people are splitting the bill.
  • Calculate: See the exact amount each person needs to contribute.

Who It Is For

Diners, groups of friends, business colleagues at lunch, and travelers adjusting to tipping cultures in different regions.

Freqently Asked Questions

Does the split include the tip?

Yes, the "Per Person" amount includes their share of the bill plus their share of the tip.

Should I tip on tax?

Etiquette varies, but generally, you tip on the pre-tax subtotal. You can enter either amount in the "Bill Amount" field depending on your preference.

Can I split unevenly?

This tool currently calculates an even split. For complex itemized splitting, you would need to calculate individual totals manually.
