Description

The BMI (Body Mass Index) Calculator is a simple yet effective tool for screening weight categories that may lead to health problems. By analyzing the relationship between your height and weight, it generates a score that categorizes you into Underweight, Normal weight, Overweight, or Obesity. While BMI is not a diagnostic tool for body fatness or health, it provides a general indication of whether your weight falls within a healthy range for your height. What It Does

It calculates a numerical value based on your weight and height input and maps it to standard BMI categories established by health organizations.

How To Use

  • Select Units: Choose between Metric (kg/cm) or Imperial (lbs/feet+inches).
  • Enter Details: Input your current weight and height accurately.
  • Calculate: Press the button to see your BMI score and corresponding category.

Who It Is For

Anyone monitoring their general physical health, fitness enthusiasts tracking progress, or individuals starting a weight management journey.

Freqently Asked Questions

Is BMI accurate for athletes?

Not always. BMI does not distinguish between muscle and fat. Athletes with high muscle mass may be categorized as "overweight" even if they have low body fat.

Does this data go to any doctor?

No. This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your health data is never saved, sent, or shared with anyone.

What is a "healthy" BMI?

Generally, a BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered normal weight, though individual health involves many more factors.
