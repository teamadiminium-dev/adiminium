We often forget exactly how old we are in terms of months or days, or we need to calculate someone's exact age for a form or legal document. This tool takes your date of birth and calculates the duration between then and today (or any specific future/past date). It accounts for leap years and the varying lengths of months to provide an accurate result. It's a fun and useful way to gain a new perspective on time.

What It Does

Calculates the precise time difference between a birthdate and the current date, outputting the result in years, months, and days.

How To Use

  • Select Birthdate: Use the date picker to choose your day, month, and year of birth.
  • Calculate: Click the button to generate your age breakdown.
  • Read Results: View your age in years, months, and days.

Who It Is For

People filling out official forms, parents tracking their baby's age in months, or anyone curious about their exact age statistics.

Freqently Asked Questions

Does it account for leap years?

Yes, the calculator logic fully accounts for leap years (listing February as 29 days) to ensure day-perfect accuracy.

Can I calculate age for a future date?

Currently, it defaults to "today," but you can interpret the result as the duration from the start date to the current moment.

Is my birthdate saved?

Absolutely not. Your birthdate is processed locally in your browser and is discarded the moment you leave or refresh the page.
