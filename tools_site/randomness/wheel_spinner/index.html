Random Wheel Spinner

Edit Segments

Description

Can't decide where to eat? Who goes first? Or just want to leave it to fate? The Adiminium Wheel Spinner is your colorful decision-making companion. Customize the wheel with up to 8 different options, then let it rip. The tool uses a physics-based easing curve to simulate a realistic spin, landing on a random segment every time.

What It Does

Spins a customizable wheel to randomly select one option from your list.

How To Use

  • Edit: Change the text in the input boxes to valid options (e.g. Yes/No, Pizza/Burger).
  • Add/Remove: Use the + and - buttons to adjust the number of segments (Min 2, Max 8).
  • Spin: Click the big button and wait for the result.

Who It Is For

Indecisive groups, teachers picking students, gamification, and party games.
