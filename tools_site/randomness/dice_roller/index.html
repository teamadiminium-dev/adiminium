3D Dice Roller

Total: -

Description

Experience the satisfaction of rolling physical dice right in your browser. This upgraded 3D Dice Roller uses advanced CSS transformations to simulate a realistic tumble. Calculate probabilities, play board games remotely, or just enjoy the animation. Each die is a fully rendered 3D cube that rotates to a random face, ensuring both visual flair and fair randomness.

How To Use

  • Select Count: Choose up to 6 dice to roll at once.
  • Roll: Click "ROLL DICE" and watch them spin.
  • Result: The total sum is calculated automatically once they land.

Who It Is For

Tabletop gamers, probability students, and anyone who prefers visual feedback over plain text numbers.
