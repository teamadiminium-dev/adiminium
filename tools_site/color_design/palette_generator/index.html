Color Palette Generator

Copied to clipboard!

Description

Choosing the right color scheme is essential for any design project, but finding harmonious colors can be tricky. The Adiminium Color Palette Generator does the heavy lifting for you. With a single click (or press of the Spacebar), it instantly generates a fresh 5-color palette. If you find a color you love, simply hit the lock icon to keep it while you reshuffle the others. This allows you to iterate and refine your palette until it's perfect. You can then copy the HEX codes directly to your clipboard for use in Figma, CSS, or any other design tool.

What It Does

Generates random 5-color palettes with the ability to lock specific colors and copy hex codes.

How To Use

  • Generate: Press the "Spacebar" or click "Generate New Palette" to randomize colors.
  • Lock: Click the "Lock" icon (open padlock) on any color to prevent it from changing.
  • Copy: Click on the HEX code (e.g., #FF5733) to copy it to your clipboard.
  • Refine: Continue generating until you have a complete set of 5 colors you like.

Who It Is For

Graphic designers, UI developers, artists, and brand strategists.

Freqently Asked Questions

How are the colors generated?

Currently, we use a completely random algorithm for maximum variety.

Can I save my palette?

This version does not support cloud saving, but you can copy the codes to a local file.

Does it support CMYK?

We primarily output web-ready HEX codes. You can use our Converters tool to switch between formats if needed.
