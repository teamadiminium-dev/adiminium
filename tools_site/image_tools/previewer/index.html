The Quality Control Workspace: Distraction-Free Previewer

The Adiminium Image Previewer is a specialized workspace for Visual Quality Assurance (QA). Standard OS photo viewers often distract you with menus, toolbars, and filmstrips. This tool offers a "Zen Mode" environment specifically designed for inspecting pixels, compression artifacts, and focus accuracy.

The Importance of "1:1" Viewing

Most modern screens (Retina, 4K) scale images automatically. A 1000px image might be displayed as 500 CSS pixels. This hides defects. Our tool allows you to view the image at true 100% scale (1 image pixel = 1 physical screen pixel), revealing:

  • Compression Artifacts: Blocky "JPEG noise" in gradients.
  • Focus Issues: Slight blur that isn't visible when zoomed out.
  • Design Alignment: Checking if a logo is perfectly centered pixel-by-pixel.

Features for Designers

  • Dark Environment: A neutral dark gray/black background ensures your color perception isn't skewed by surrounding white UI brightness.
  • Infinite Panning: Click and drag to navigate huge diagrams or infographics freely.
  • Zoom Steps: Use the +/- buttons to jump between standard zoom levels (50%, 100%, 200%).

Who Should Use This?

  • Web Designers: Inspecting exported assets before deployment.
  • Photographers: "Chimping" (checking focus) on exported JPEGs.
  • Digital Artists: Checking line work and anti-aliasing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why not just use Chrome's default viewer?

When you drag an image into a new Chrome tab, it places it on a white background and shrinks it to fit the window (`max-width: 100%`). You have to click to zoom, but you can't control the specific zoom level. Our tool gives you precise control.
